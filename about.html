What is AniRoyaleX?
 AniRoyaleX is a Free to Play battle Royale styled game, with a few twists.
Instead of the usual Gun game fight, we’ve replaced them with characters, and added a gacha system to obtain them
We’ll also be adding the option to give customization to each character, making each players playstyle unique to them.
We plan on adding all sorts of characters form multiple different shows and media, and plan on involving the community to make sure that the game is enjoyed by all.
